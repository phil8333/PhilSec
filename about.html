About PhilSec

PhilSec is a modern cybersecurity platform built to provide reliable information, curated tools, tutorials, downloads, and real-world updates from the world of cyber defense.

Our mission is to make cybersecurity more accessible — whether you're a student, aspiring analyst, digital privacy enthusiast, or seasoned professional.

What We Do

  • Provide breakdowns of cybersecurity news, threats, and vulnerabilities.
  • Review and curate free & open-source security tools.
  • Publish guides on hardening Windows, Linux, and mobile devices.
  • Offer downloadable resources such as checklists, tools, and templates.

Why PhilSec Exists

Cybersecurity information is scattered, overly technical, or behind paywalls. PhilSec aims to bring clarity, simplicity, and accessibility to everyone — especially in the Philippines and the global community of learners.

Founder
